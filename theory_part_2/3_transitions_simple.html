  • Adjust scaling, opacity and duration with inline modifiers together with x-show.

Example: Inline Transition + Modifiers with x-show

Example

  1. Together with x-show you can use .transition to add an animation.
  2. The transition can be modified in duration in milliseconds and scale in percent). With .opacity you can switch the opacity transition on and off.
  3. Example:
    x-show.transition.duration.300ms.scale.80.opacity="visible"
